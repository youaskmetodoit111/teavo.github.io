Pre-Cellular Age

This section explores the foundations of telecommunications before mobile devices existed.

Key Technologies

  • Rotary Telephones
  • Landline Networks
  • Public Payphones
  • Telegraph Systems
  • Pagers and Beepers

Notable Events

  • 1876 – Invention of the telephone by Alexander Graham Bell
  • 1930s – Rotary dials become widespread
  • 1960s – Touch-tone dialing introduced
  • 1970s – Rise of pagers in hospitals and businesses
